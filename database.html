Raptor Data Base Access

What is the Data Base

The data base is the central location where all the monitoring information is entered by the motoring groups of each park with in Colorado, that has a monitoring program in place .The data that is gathered will be used for park management and other state agency that have access to the information to do reports and make good decisions about how to proceed with certain projects and also be able to give a good idea of if the echo system is healthy or not.

How do I use the Data Base

The date base is fairly easy to use all you basically have to do is log in and follow the field data sheet that you fill out when you are out monitoring the raptors and enter the information as you see it on the field forms and submit it and continue on entering the data .The database is also a good tool to use to see how other parks are doing and how successful they are doing.

Data Base Access

In order to get access to the data base you got to go thru the training class that CPW will do with you once you sign up to be a monitor. Once the training is done they will give you the credential to log in to the database so you can begin you adventure as a raptor monitor. Î©
