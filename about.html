About Us

Colorado Raptor Montoring Project (C.R.M.P) began it journey in 2013 by Marcus Finning he wanted to be come a falconer but did not have the money or time need to become a falconer.so the next bet thing was to promote the birds of pray and try to get the communities around him to make them aware of the beautiful creatures of the sky.We are non profit and manly function on a valenter bases with the cooperation with Colorado Division of Parks and Wildlife or (CDPW)for short.
